Rebecca Gallop

BSc (Hons) · MSTO

Advanced Level 5 Clinical Sports Therapist, Health Coach and Medical Acupuncturist.

Rebecca has always loved studying the human body, a fascination that took her to the University of Sheffield, where she graduated with First Class Honours in Biomedical Science before building a career in the medical devices industry.

She found SPORTS CLINICAL as a patient. Struggling with a long-term injury of her own, and close to giving up after trying numerous different practices, she came to Dean, who got her back running. The holistic, whole-body approach reignited her passion for the human body, and she returned to education to train as a Level 5 Sports Therapist and Medical Acupuncture Practitioner, joining SPORTS CLINICAL in 2017.

A special interest in running injuries

Rebecca is a keen marathon runner with a personal best of 2:43, achieved since having her first baby, which is why running injuries are a special interest. Her current research and study focus on Health Coaching and Lifestyle Medicine.

Professional standing

Qualified and trusted

Rebecca holds First Class Honours in Biomedical Science from the University of Sheffield and qualified as an Advanced Level 5 Clinical Sports Therapist, with further qualifications as a Health Coach and a Medical Acupuncture Practitioner. She is a Member of the Sports Therapy Organisation and her expertise has been part of the practice since 2017.
