What it's for

Precision with a light touch

Medical Acupuncture helps with persistent muscle pain, deep-seated tension and tightness that has not fully responded to other approaches, used on its own or woven into your wider treatment. It is suitable for most people, regardless of age or ability, and both of our practitioners are qualified in its use, so it is always applied by the person who already knows your case.

Every session starts with a rigorous assessment of whether acupuncture is right for you, with everything explained and your written consent taken before a single needle is used. The needles themselves are fine, sterile and single-use, and most people find the experience far gentler than they expected, often noticeably relaxing. Its place in your plan is reviewed as you progress, alongside hands-on treatment and rehabilitation.
