New patient intake

Once your appointment is confirmed, we send you a short form to complete beforehand, so your first visit is about you, not paperwork.

Before your first visit

Everything ready before you arrive

When you get in touch and we have agreed a time, we send you a secure link to your intake form. You complete it in your own time and return it before your first consultation.

It comes back to us privately and is held safely within our clinical records, seen only by the practitioner caring for you. Having it in advance means we can read your history properly ahead of the day, and spend your appointment on you rather than on forms.

Not sure what the visit itself involves? Read What to expect.

How it works

Three simple steps

1

Arrange your visit

Get in touch through Contact and booking, and we will find a time that suits you.

2

Complete your form

We email you a secure link once your appointment is confirmed. Fill it in and return it before you come, and it is stored safely in our records.

3

Bring your history

On the day, bring any medical correspondence you have: GP or consultant letters, scan or imaging results, any blood test results, and a note of any medication or supplements. If you have nothing to hand, that is fine; we will take a full history with you.

Your privacy

Your information is kept secure and confidential, held only within our clinical records and never shared.
