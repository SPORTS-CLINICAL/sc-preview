From the clinic

How we work, and why

Short notes on how the clinic runs, written to answer the questions we hear most often.

Two practitioners, one approach

Dean and Rebecca each have their own patients and their own way of working, and between them they cover a wide range of ground. What they share is the approach: understand the whole person first, treat the cause rather than chase the symptom, and be honest about what care can and cannot do. If one of us is better placed to help you, we will say so.

Why we do not put a booking calendar online

A calendar would be easier for us, but it would not be better for you. We plan each week deliberately, so that first consultations get the time they need and follow-up care lands when it will do the most good. Send us an enquiry with a rough idea of when you are free, and we will find you a time that works properly rather than one that simply had a gap in it.

The part you do between visits

The work in the treatment room matters, and so does everything that happens in the days after it. We tend to send people away with a small amount to do, and we keep it small on purpose: something achievable beats something ideal that never gets done. It is one reason we do not sell packages of a fixed number of sessions.

Looking after your records

Everything we hold about you stays with us. Your notes, your history and your consent forms live in our own secure system, encrypted, kept only as long as we are required to keep them, and never sold, shared or handed on to anyone without your say so.
