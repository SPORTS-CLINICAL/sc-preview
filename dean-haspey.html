Dean Haspey

Clinical Lead and Neuro-Musculoskeletal Practitioner.

Bio · approved copy · warm, human, credible · sidebar keeps the booking route visible

Dean's fascination with the human form, its remarkable capacity and capability, began young: curious about how the body worked and moved, an interest that grew through dance, sport and martial arts into the formal study of human medicine, the brain and neurological functioning.

The drive to know more, and to make sense of what others leave unexplained, means Dean has never stopped studying and researching, training with some of the leading authorities in the field. He specialises in all aspects of musculoskeletal therapy, neurological functioning, functional and lifestyle medicine.
